Harissa Pizza

₹350

Our crowning culinary achievement that bridges borders. Slow-cooked Kashmiri mutton harissa — a beloved winter wazwan delicacy — layered over hand-stretched sourdough with fresh mozzarella, red onions, and local saffron spices. Each slice is an elegant tribute to local heritage and global craft.

"The Story Behind the Dish: Created during a freezing Srinagar winter, when we sought to bring the deep warmth of traditional clay-pot wazwan harissa to the shared comfort of a hot wood-fired pizza."

Czochwaur Burger

₹280

Named after the traditional Kashmiri spice blend and bread craft, this burger features a handcrafted mutton patty heavily seasoned with local wazwan herbs. Topped with caramelized onions, crispy lettuce, and our secret herb-chutney mayonnaise, all sandwiched inside a soft local-style bun.

"The Story Behind the Dish: An homage to the local street vendors of Srinagar. We wanted to elevate the humble street snack into a gourmet dining burger while retaining its core aromatic profile."
Order Czochwaur Burger

Kashmiri Kahwa

₹120

More than a drink — it is a hospitality ritual. Handpicked local green tea leaves steeped slowly with real Kashmiri saffron strands, cinnamon bark, cardamom pods, and crushed sweet almonds. Served warm in matching glassware, it delivers the true essence of valley hospitality.

"The Story Behind the Dish: Every Kashmiri household starts and ends its day with Kahwa. Our recipe replicates the exact blend passed down through generations in the wazwan waza families."
Order Kahwa Now
