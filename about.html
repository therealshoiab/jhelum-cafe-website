Heritage

Our Story

Crafting memories on the river bank, where Kashmiri wazwan blends with global cafe vibes.

Nestled on the banks of the historic Jhelum River, right at the iconic Budshah Bridge in the heart of Lal Chowk, Jhelum Cafe & Fine Dine is Srinagar's beloved two-storey culinary retreat. Since opening our doors, we have become a cherished gathering place where the warmth of Kashmiri hospitality meets the creativity of modern cafe culture.

"We don't just cook food; we cook stories that span generations, bridging the gap between grandmother's kitchen and global culinary boards."

Our ground floor cafe bursts with artistic energy — colorful walls, cozy corners, and the soothing aroma of freshly brewed Kashmiri Kahwa. Upstairs, discover our vibrant cafe space where friends gather over signature burgers and fusion pizzas. Below, step into an elegant fine-dining basement where traditional Kashmiri masterpieces like Rogan Josh and Yakhni are served with the ceremony they deserve.

2021

Opening Our Doors

Inaugurated our riverside dining space at Lal Chowk, establishing Jhelum Cafe as Srinagar's newest hotspot.

2022

Introducing Wazwan Fusion

Introduced the iconic Czochwaur Burger and Harissa Pizza, blending wazwan spice heritage with classic fast food.

2023

Base Fine Dining Expansion

Renovated our cozy basement into an upscale Kashmiri fine-dining chamber for family dinners and banquets.
