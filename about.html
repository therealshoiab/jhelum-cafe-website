About Us

Where Kashmir Meets Your Cup — Since Day One

Our Story

A Culinary Journey on the Banks of Jhelum

Where tradition flows with the river, and every meal is a celebration.

Nestled on the banks of the historic Jhelum River, at the iconic Budshah Bridge in the heart of Lal Chowk, Jhelum Cafe & Fine Dine is Srinagar's beloved two-storey culinary wonder. Since opening our doors, we have become a cherished gathering place where the warmth of Kashmiri hospitality meets the creativity of modern cafe culture.

Our ground floor cafe bursts with artistic energy — colorful walls, cozy corners, and the aroma of freshly brewed Kashmiri Kahwa. Upstairs, discover our vibrant cafe space where friends gather over signature burgers and fusion pizzas. Below, step into an elegant fine-dining basement where traditional Kashmiri masterpieces like Rogan Josh and Yakhni are served with the ceremony they deserve.

Every dish at Jhelum Cafe tells a story — from our legendary Harissa Special Pizza that bridges Kashmir and Italy, to the beloved Czochwaur Burger that has become a Srinagar icon. We source fresh ingredients daily and blend time-honored Kashmiri recipes with global flavors, creating a menu that surprises and delights with every bite.

A wonderful place to hangout with friends and to go out with family. Sweet blend of a serene ambience, an innovative menu and a delightfully welcoming staff.

— Adil K., Google Review
