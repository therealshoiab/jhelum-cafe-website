Everything you need to know about dining, reservation, and delivery options at Jhelum Cafe.

📅 Table Reservations

You can use our table reservation form on the Contact page. Submitting it will automatically compose a WhatsApp reservation query to our front desk for fast confirmation.

No, reserving a table is completely free of charge. You only pay for the food and drinks you order during your visit.

We hold reserved tables for up to 15 minutes past the scheduled time. If you expect to be later, please call us to keep your booking active.

Yes, we accommodate large groups. We recommend messaging our WhatsApp desk at least 24 hours in advance so we can prepare your table setting.

Yes! Walk-ins are always welcome. However, since we get busy on weekends, booking a table is highly recommended to avoid wait times.

🛵 Delivery & Takeaway

We are officially registered on Zomato for online deliveries in Srinagar. Simply search for "Jhelum Cafe" in the Zomato app to place your order.

You can use the WhatsApp Order Builder on our Menu page. It builds your cart list and formats it into a direct text message sent straight to our WhatsApp desk.

Our average home delivery time is 35-50 minutes, depending on the distance from Lal Chowk and traffic conditions in Srinagar.

Yes! We accept Cash on Delivery (COD) as well as UPI payments (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm) for direct orders placed via WhatsApp.

Yes! All our traditional wazwan main courses and fusion pizzas are securely packed in premium food-grade packaging to keep them fresh and hot.

🏢 About the Cafe

We are situated at Budshah Bridge, Maisuma, Lal Chowk, Srinagar, J&K 190001. We sit directly on the river bank, offering fantastic scenery.

We are open every day from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM, seven days a week, including national and local holidays.

Yes, street parking is available along Lal Chowk. We recommend arriving early during peak weekend dinner hours or using local parking bays.

No, Jhelum Cafe & Fine Dine is a strictly non-smoking indoor dining venue. We maintain clean, fresh air for all our patrons.

Our ground floor cafe entrance has flat step-free access, which accommodates wheelchairs. However, our first-floor balcony and basement dining spaces are accessible via stairs only.
